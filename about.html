Our story...

Transcendent Beauty by Tahlia, founded in Australia, Victoria, in the city of Melbourne. Is a beauty brand that wants everyone to feel beautiful inside and out! Founded in 2024, Transcendent Beauty makes sure they have the latest trends of beauty tips to share with their customers and offer a wide range of beauty products. Transcendent beauty believes that beauty is ‘Beyond a physical experience’ and can change the attitude and mood within one application. Felling beautiful on the inside allows us to know we are beautiful on the outside!

Our definition of beautiful…

Transcendent Beauty by Tahlia, strongly believe that beautiful is not just what you can physically see. It means that you show beauty towards other people, being kind and openminded, ignoring all sorts of negativity and embracing imperfections confidently. Beautiful souls appear more beautiful on the outside if anything. Beauty is indeed, ‘Beyond a physical experience.
